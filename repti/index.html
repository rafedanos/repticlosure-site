Technical Specs

ReptiClosure combines a modern hood, durable body, and smart internals to create a compact yet capable habitat.

  • Interior Volume: ~29.5 gallons
  • Interior Dimensions: L 12.5 in × W 16 in × H 34 in
  • Materials: Light, waterproof PVC for body; optimized hood design
  • Ventilation: Multi‑fan layout with filtered intake & direct exhaust
  • Lighting: Full‑spectrum LEDs with optional UVB/UVA diodes
  • Heating: Precision IR bulb with protective mesh + thermostat control
  • Misting: Filtered basin, quiet pump, dual mist heads
  • Interface: On‑device display and buttons; app‑free adjustments
